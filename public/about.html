About Us

Discover the story behind Latter Glory Academy and our commitment to building tomorrow's leaders through excellence in education.

Our Story

Our History

Latter Glory Academy was founded on September 5, 2011, with a vision to revolutionize private education in Ogbomoso and its environs. Our goal is to provide a nurturing and innovative learning environment that inspires students to achieve their full potential. Over the years, we have grown from a small institution to a renowned center of academic excellence, consistently producing outstanding students who go on to excel in higher institutions and various career paths.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide world-class education that nurtures the intellectual, moral, and physical development of every child. We are committed to creating a safe, supportive, and stimulating environment where students can discover their potential, develop critical thinking skills, and become responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Nigeria and beyond, recognized for academic excellence, character development, and innovation. We envision producing generations of confident, creative, and compassionate leaders who will shape the future of our nation and the world at large.

Our Core Values

Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in all we do.

Integrity: Building character through honest and ethical behavior.

Innovation: Embracing creativity and forward thinking.

Teamwork: Fostering collaboration and mutual respect.

Discipline: Instilling self-control and responsibility.
